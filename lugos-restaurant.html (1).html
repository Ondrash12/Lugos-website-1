About Us

Our story of bringing authentic Mexican flavors to Michigan

Lugo's is a family-owned restaurant bringing authentic recipes from Jalisco, Mexico, to Michigan. Since opening our first location in Bad Axe in 2020, we've expanded to Caro and Sandusky, with a fourth location coming soon to Harbor Beach.

Our success is built on authentic family recipes and a welcoming atmosphere that keeps people coming back. We've grown thanks to our delicious food and the comfort found in authentic Mexican cuisine.

Now serving the Upper Thumb area with the true taste of Jalisco, Mexico.
